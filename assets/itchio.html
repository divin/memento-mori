Overview

Reflect on the preciousness of time with "memento mori" for Playdate.

This simple application provides a perspective on your estimated lifespan based on statistical data. After an initial setup, it displays:

  • Your estimated life expectancy.
  • The approximate time remaining in years, months, weeks, and days.
  • The percentage of your estimated life already lived.

Additionally, the app features a "lockscreen" mode. In this mode, the screen update rate is reduced to 1 frame per second (FPS), and the Playdate's automatic screen lock is disabled. This allows the display to remain on continuously with minimal battery consumption.

Privacy Note: The information required for setup (country, gender, and date of birth) is stored only locally on your Playdate device. It is never transmitted or shared.

Data Source: Life expectancy estimates are based on data provided by the World Health Organization (WHO).

Controls

  • Use the D-pad to navigate options during setup.
  • Press the A button to confirm selections.
  • Optionally use the Crank to scroll through lists or adjust values.
  • The B button may be used to go back in menus (if applicable).

Installing

    1. Download the .pdx file.
    2. Log in to your Playdate Account at play.date/account.
    3. Go to the Sideload page.
    4. Drag and drop the downloaded .pdx file (or zipped file containing it) onto the upload area, or use the button to select the file.
    5. On your Playdate device, navigate to Settings → Games → Refresh list to find and download the app.
    6. Launch Memento Mori from your Playdate's main menu.

    Having issues? Check out the official Playdate sideloading guide from Panic.
